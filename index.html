Twin Zipliane

The most unique attraction from the top of the hill straight to the activity center. The level of difficulty is
low, but the view is breath-taking. Buckle up and glide over the wadi a friend in our twin 350 – meter
zip-line

Wall Clibing

A special constructed climbing wall has recessed and projecting hold to provide practice to the back
of the main zip-line tower, the climbing wall features 4-5 lanes with varying difficult levels for guests of all ages.

Gate Price :



Duration: 15 minutes

Cannon

This exhilarating experience involves a catapult that is designed to achieve speeds of nearly
100-km/hour for thrill-seekers who want more than bungee jumping. Unlike a bungee, which
involves a free fall and rebound, the human slingshot pushes guests straight out in one
direction before dropping them.

Price :



Age: 16+

Drop In

The most unique attraction to Hatta Wadi Hub. Unleash your adrenaline
as you launch from multiple slide straight into a plunge pool allowing you to reach heights of 15
meters and speeds of 40 – 80 kmph. The thrill mixed with the view is breathtaking. Gear up, grab
your board and race a friend to the bottom.



Gate Price :




Axe Throwing

Another first in the UAE and the region, the axe throwing experience is run by experts in
of training guests on safety and accuracy. Guests spend half an hour becoming intimate
with 680g. of a piece of whirling wood and metal as they learn, practice and finally square
off on the leaderboard.


Gate Price :



Duration: 15 minutes

Archery

Archery, a favorite sport of the UAE, can be enjoyed at a specially
built zone featuring five lanes and a target distance of 10-m. this is exciting
for every skill level, from the first –time archer to the most experienced.


Gate Price :



Duration: 15 minutes

Net Walkway

Walk through the Wadi on suspended footbridges. The canopy creates fun lookouts over natural sites allowing users
to look down as they are suspended midair. A relaxing way to appreciate Hatta’s natural beauty.


Gate Price :



Duration: 15 minutes

Mountain Cart

Experience Hatta’s newest gravity – fueled activity – mountain carting! Take a leisure roll or race your friends
on these wheeled downhill carts and enjoy the view as you glide down the mountain side marveling at the
picturesque scenes of Hatta. No prior experience is needed for this activity, just a sense of adventure & a thirst for fun.


Price :



Duration: 1 round
